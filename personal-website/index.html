Welcome to Angela S. Milford's Heritage Website

Talofa lava and Bulavinaka! In English, Greetings and Welcome! My name is Angela S. Milford, but I am commonly and friendly known as "Teph." I am 19 years old, and I was born and raised in the smallest U.S. Territory in the Pacific: American Samoa. My ancestry ties are in two Pacific Island nations: Samoa and Fiji. On this website, you will get a feel for what life is like in both Samoa and Fiji. I am proud to share with you about my two countries!
