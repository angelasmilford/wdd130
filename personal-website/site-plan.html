Overview

Purpose

To provide you with a better understanding of me by exploring my heritage. Many people are unaware of the Pacific, let alone its island nations. I hope that after exploring this website, you will have a clear understanding of the Pacific and develop an interest in learning about the different nations here in the Pacific.

Audience

This website is intended for those who are interested in learning more about me, both culturally and personally.
